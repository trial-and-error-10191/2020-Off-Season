Meet the team.

In June of 2015 our team was formed by Cody Goss, before the 2015/2016 ResQ game.
Our team has been located in Vicksburg, Mississippi since the beginning.
We have competed in state competitions every year that we’ve been a team, and we
have won six different awards including the Control Award, the Connect Award,
and the Rockwell Collins Innovate Award. At the beginning of our team we were
operating out of a toolbox, and we had no official meeting place. However, a new
workshop soon became available, and we got a place to continue our shenanigans.
It was a small and cramped one room set-up, but it was something we were proud of.
After a while we were able to move into a two room set up at ERDC’s workforce development
center. During the Rover Ruckus season we were able to purchase our first game field,
so we can better test our robot. Currently our setup includes 3 main rooms, a storage closet,
and a feild room. Today our team consists of 3 adult leaders, and 7 team members.
